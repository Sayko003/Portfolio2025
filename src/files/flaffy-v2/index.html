Game story

Have you ever heard of the Mighty Acorn? At the beginning of the Triassic period, there was a Great Conflict that continues to this day. No one will say exactly what was the reason to start. Someone says that Noir started all this. She was always considered a witch and tried to avoid, but not Armand. He wasn't afraid of anyone. The Great Conflict began with the enmity between them.

Different families of squirrels have rallied into classes. Each class of these animals owns its own territory, defending and capturing new ones. Get ready for an exciting adventure in the new p2e game on the carbon-neutral WAX blockchain!

classes

Noir

“Magic, magic, magic, oh, what a bore, leave it for the nerds. May witchcraft be!”

Noir has shown a tendency to disobedience since childhood, and was interested in quite strange things for a little girl, but who would play with dead animals? The witch grew, and her powers grew with her. So, she became the strongest necromancer on the continent.

Armand

"If my father had seen you fighting, he would have blindfolded me.”

From an early age, Armand was brought up by his father, Anaxandrid, he instilled in Armand everything that a true king should have: nobility, courage, honor, devotion. All this became Armand's credo. His fighting style made his fighters one of the most elite swordsmen.

Emil

"Ahem, do you have a coin?"

The slums of the continent are one of the most unfortunate places. Thieves, robbery, murders were just a "paradise" for little Emil. Under the cover of night, amid all the outrages, he learned from the examples of others how not to do wrong. His speed and intelligence were ideally suited for such conditions, but his main goal is coins. Oh, yes, he will fulfill any order for coins, and he will do it quickly.

Bo

“I'll show them all, be sure!”

The continent is overcrowded with districts. Some of them are no different from ours. Squirrels go to school, study, and then choose their own path. Bo hated studying. Squirrels-classmates did not give him a pass, made jokes, bullied and everything like that. He dreamed of becoming one of the soldiers of the Order of Armand, but by an absurd accident he created his own clan.

Lucen

"My dear, bring me some Mora Tapinella, a demonic mushroom, and a herbal pod."

Alchemy and magic. Lucen knows everything in this area. In his youth, he was the king's right-hand man. One day, synthesizing another potion in his laboratory, he inhaled vapors of a hitherto unknown substance, and, as they say, went crazy. First, he lost his place next to the royal chambers, and then moved to the Dense Forest.

Felix

"And what are you going to do?" someone asked him.
"I'll kill them all."

Felix's story begins in one of the gladiator schools. As you know, for the most part, being a gladiator means being a slave, but Felix was in Ludus Magnus by his own will. The taste of victory... Could there be anything better than this? To come out victorious every time, and to bathe in gold for a new victory.
"So, who's next?"

how to play

PVE

Choose one of three difficulty levels, and send your squirrels on a journey, in which you can get in addition to tokens: equipment, potions, runes.

PVP

Three days a week there is a collection of applications for registration to participate in the PVP mode. The rest of the days there is a war of players. Every day you will have three attempts to attack your opponent. The number of points you score will determine your place. Participants share half of the prize pool, the other half is burned.

QUEST

There are also three modes in this adventure. Each of them has a hundred floors. For each 10th floor passed, you will find a chest with an excellent reward in order to pump or dress your squirrels. And also to get the tokens of the Mighty Acorn. Every 30 days, the tree changes its location and all progress burns down.
